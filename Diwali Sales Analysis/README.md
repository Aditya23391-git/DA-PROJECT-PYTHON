# 📊 Diwali Sales Analysis

### Exploratory Data Analysis using Python


Transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights


---

## 🚀 Overview

This project performs an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on Diwali sales data to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify high-value segments, and uncover trends that can drive better business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics (Age, Gender, Marital Status)
* Identify top-performing regions and states
* Evaluate product category performance
* Detect high-revenue customer segments
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* Python
* Pandas, NumPy
* Matplotlib, Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Analysis & Key Questions

* What is the distribution of customers by gender?
* Which gender contributes more to total sales?
* Which age group has the highest purchasing power?
* Which states generate the most revenue?
* Which occupations spend the most?
* Which product categories perform best?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 👩 Female customers contribute the highest share of revenue
* 🎯 Age group **26–35** is the most active buyer segment
* 💍 Married customers show higher purchasing behavior
* 🏙️ A few states dominate overall sales contribution
* 🛍️ Certain product categories drive maximum revenue

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Target **female customers aged 26–35**
* Focus on **high-performing states**
* Promote **top-selling product categories**
* Create personalized offers for high-value segments
